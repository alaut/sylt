## Wakefields

$$V_{W} = -\frac{i}{\omega_s}\frac{Z}{n}\frac{d\lambda}{d\tau}$$

$$\frac{Z}{n}=-j\frac{Z_o}{\beta_s\gamma_s^2}{g}$$

## Induced Voltage

$$V'_{SC}(\tau) = \frac{g}{\omega_s}\frac{Z_0}{\beta_s\gamma_s^2}\frac{d^2\lambda}{d\tau^2}$$
